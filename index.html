Federation custom dictates that the lowest card deals. In a society where rank governs every transaction, the small task of distributing the deck falls to the citizen of weakest standing — status determines service, and service is the order. The hands you are dealt may prove unjust, but the dealing itself, at least, is honest enough.
You'll play one Mission per player, dealer rotating each time. Total scores across all Missions decide who becomes Commander of the Liberator. Your table will be filled out with background crew & opportunists pulled from the wider Rebellion — they'll talk. A lot.
